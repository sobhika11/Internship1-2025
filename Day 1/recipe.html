Ingredients

For the omelette mixture:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped tomato
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • Â¼ teaspoon black pepper or red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves (optional)

For the bread:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 teaspoon butter or oil (for toasting)

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat well.
  2. Add chopped onion, tomato, green chili, salt, pepper, and coriander. Mix everything.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan and add butter/oil.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and spread evenly.
  5. Place the bread slices directly on the wet omelette while it's still cooking.
  6. Press gently and let it cook for 1-2 minutes on medium flame.
  7. Flip everything together so the bread gets toasted on the other side too.
  8. Cook until both sides are golden and omelette is cooked through.
  9. Remove and serve hot with ketchup or chutney!
