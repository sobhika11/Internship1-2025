My fav thriller movies

Ratchasan

Ratchasan is a 2018 Indian Tamil-language psychological thriller film written and directed by Ram Kumar. The film stars Vishnu Vishal, Amala Paul, and Saravanan in lead roles. It follows a cop who investigates a series of murders linked to a serial killer.

Interstellar

Interstellar is a 2014 epic science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The film stars Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, and Jessica Chastain. It explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the survival of humanity as astronauts travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for mankind.

Eleven

Eleven is a 2016 Indian Malayalam-language thriller film directed by Rajesh Pillai. The film stars Prithviraj Sukumaran, Indrajith Sukumaran, and Parvathy Thiruvothu. It revolves around a group of friends who get caught up in a series of mysterious events during a road trip.
